WHY TAXIS?
  • Taxi advertisement started already more than 10 years ago in cities like New York, Paris and London. Taxis drive 15-20 hours a day and come into every corner of the city. In many big cities, the traffic is very busy and the taxis are driving slow or even standing still, this is a big plus for the ads on the taxi.


  • Taxis are a practical part of everyday city life and are used and seen by financial high-fliers, business decision makers, shoppers, wealthy residents, tourists, household purchasers, passers-by and other motorists.

  • Taxi advertising is a very cost-efficient marketing tool for its users - Compare to other conventional method of advertising like newspapers, radio, TV and billboards, taxi advertising has a higher audience reach and impact. It has the ability to reach part of the city that is difficult or impossible to reach by other traditional means of advertising at a fraction of the cost.

  • Taxi advertising stands out as the most powerful technique in marketing and advertising. This moving outdoor display advertising is constantly sending your message to the public and unceasingly putting your brand in front of your customers anytime of the day.
